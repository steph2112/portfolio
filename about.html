About me

Hi! I’m Steph Briceño Flores a Product Designer with a strong interest in data driven decision making. Recently, I completed a Data Analytics course to deepen my understanding of how insights can shape better user experiences and more strategic product outcomes. I believe great design is not just intuitive it's informed.

I started my career in Telecommunications Engineering, which gave me a solid foundation in problem-solving. My background includes working at Ogilvy, whereI collaborated with clients from diverse industries. That experience taught me how to adapt quickly, understand different business goals, and create solutions that balance user needs with stakeholder expectations. I’ve worked across multidisciplinary teams, from strategy and tech to creative, which has improved both my communication and problem-solving skills.

Today, I’m focused on building meaningful digital products with a solid foundation in test A/B and data. I’m especially interested in roles where I can contribute throughout the entire product lifecycle from discovery to delivery while continuing to grow as a designer who understands not just how things look, but why they work.
