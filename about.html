About Me

UI/UX Designer & Telecommunication Engineer


I'm Stephanie, a problem-solving enthusiast, curious and empathetic, who turns challenges into user-centered solutions. I graduated in Telecommunications Engineering and have experience as a UX/UI designer, business analyst, and QA.

Working at Visa Argentina allowed me to understand the entire process of product design, and it was there that I discovered UX/UI design, which I became so passionate about that I decided to study it.

My experience at Ogilvy gave me a wealth of insights on how to work with a focus on delivering quality designs and solutions for multiple projects that required specialized solutions tailored to their specific needs.

I believe in research, ideation, rapid prototyping, and user testing as a work philosophy.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling to inspiring places and reading fantasy and science fiction books.
